About

I was inspired to develop this website as a tool for housing navigation from my prior experience as a Housing Specialist at a non-profit where my role was to assist individuals experiencing homelessness in obtaining long-term housing. This web application was created to assist those with limited resources in communicating their needs for housing to their landlords, whether it be drafting a well-written letter of a tenant's rights violation, a letter of advocacy for an individual with a criminal background, or just answering general fair housing questions. Users can copy and paste the AI-generated responses in their correspondents to their landlord or housing provider. Tenant rights are frequently overlooked simply because tenants do not know their rights or how to properly communicate their concerns to their housing providers, and they oftentimes fall back into a cycle of homelessness because of it.

What exactly does this website do?

This site uses artificial intelligence from ChatGPT's API to generate letter templates, housing resources, or answer general housing law questions. The below templates are prompts that I have used directly with my past clients.

How to use:

Copy and paste the question template into the terminal below, change any necessary details specific to your situation, and hit enter. Use the response for your own benefit!

Suggested Question Templates:

  • What is the rent control law in [insert your city, state]
  • Write a general letter to my potential landlord explaining why I would be a good tenant.
  • [insert your housing voucher program] is paying for my rent. Write a letter to my potential landlord explaining that.
  • Write a letter of advocacy to my potential landlord. I have a criminal background with a [insert your criminal charge] charge.
  • Write a persuasive email addressed to my potential landlord explaining why they should select a tenant enrolled in [insert housing voucher provider/program] instead of someone who is not enrolled in [insert housing voucher provider/program]
  • What are the ADA laws for rental units in [City, State]
  • My rental unit is not compliant with ADA regulations. Write a letter to my landlord or property manager adressing these concerns.
  • I feel that my landlord is discriminating against me because of [insert reason]. Write a letter to my landlord addressing this.
  • What is the law for a landlord to return a security deposit in [city, state]?
  • My landlord has not returned my security deposit. I live in [city, state]. Write a letter to my landlord adressing this.
